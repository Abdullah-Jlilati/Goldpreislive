Information about the protection of your data

The protection of your personal data is very important to us. This website is solely for displaying gold and silver prices as well as financial information for users in Europe.

1. Collection and Processing of Data

When you visit our website, information is automatically collected by the web server (e.g., IP address, browser type, time). This data is used exclusively for the technical provision of the website and to improve our services.

2. Cookies

This website uses cookies to improve user experience and to display personalized advertising through Google AdSense. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

You can restrict the use of cookies in your browser settings. Please note that some features of the website may not be fully available as a result.

3. Google AdSense

Google AdSense uses cookies and web beacons. Google may collect information about visits to this website to provide relevant advertisements. This information may be transmitted to servers in the USA.

For more information about data use by Google, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

4. GDPR Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: You have the right to know what data we store about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data.
  • Right to Restriction: You can request restriction of processing of your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to receive your data in a common format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your data.

5. Contact

If you have questions about data protection, please contact us via our contact page.

6. Changes

We reserve the right to change this privacy policy at any time. The current version is always available on this page.
